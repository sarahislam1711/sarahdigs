Every Services Landing Page Should Include

(And the layout that converts best)

Below is the ideal scroll journey for a high-performing service page.

1. Hero Section (Above The Fold)

Goal: Instantly tell them what you do and who it’s for.
Must include:

A sharp value proposition (1–2 lines)

A supporting line that clarifies the transformation

One primary CTA (“Book a consultation”, “Start your strategy”, etc.)

Optional small proof snippet: “Trusted by X founders” or client logos (only if aesthetic)

Example structure:

Brand strategy that sharpens your positioning and grows your revenue.
For founders who need clarity, direction and a strategic plan that makes their marketing actually work.
[Start Your Strategy Call]

2. Quick Diagnostic: “Is this for you?”

Goal: Self-qualification → boosts conversion by 28% on average.

A short bullet list like:

You have traction but no clear direction

Marketing feels reactive instead of planned

Your brand feels scattered or outdated

You want clarity around where the profit opportunities actually are

This makes people feel “omg she understands me”.

3. The Core Promise (Your Service in One Sentence)

Goal: State the outcome, not the features.

Something like:

I help you identify opportunities, define your strategic direction, elevate your brand, and turn your business into a structured, scalable growth engine.

4. What’s Inside the Service (But SUPER Short)

Goal: Show the process, but keep it light.
People don’t read paragraphs → they skim.

Use a 3–5 step visual model:

Discovery & Audit

Insight & Opportunities

Brand & Positioning

Strategic Roadmap

Execution Support (optional)

You don’t need to go deep—just enough to reduce anxiety and build trust.

5. Expected Outcomes / Transformation

Goal: Show what will change after working with you.

High-conversion phrasing focuses on:

clarity

organization

confidence

direction

consistency

predictability

increased revenue potential

differentiated positioning

Use bullets like:

A clear brand position you can explain in one sentence

A roadmap for the next 6–12 months

Pinpointed growth opportunities

Messaging that resonates

A cohesive brand you feel proud of

Better decisions → more profit

6. Proof (the conversion engine)

Even 1 piece of social proof increases conversion by 40–60%.

Include one or more:

Selected testimonials

Before/after examples

Case study highlights

Mini stats like: “Client grew organic traffic by 220% in 6 months”

Logos of brands you’ve helped

Keep proof short, clean, and super visual.

7. Pricing or “How to Start”

Depending on your strategy:

If high ticket → don’t list prices

If scalable offer → show package previews

Either way:
Add a “What happens next” 3-step onboarding:

Book a call

We evaluate needs

We begin your brand & strategy roadmap

Helps reduce friction.

8. CTA Section (Final Push)

A repeat of the hero CTA, plus reassurance copy.

Example:

Ready to transform your brand into a clear, high-performing growth engine?
[Start Your Strategy Call]

Add micro-proof under the button:

No pressure, no obligations — just clarity.
