2. “WHAT YOU GET WHEN YOU WORK WITH ME” SECTION (Your Core Ask)

This replaces traditional “What I offer” / “Packages” sections.

Design Approach

Large, clean, 3-column (desktop) or scrollable interactive cards (mobile).

Section Title

What to Expect When We Build Your Custom Strategy

3–5 Cards Only — short, punchy, easy to skim
Card 1 — Deep Discovery

Mini-audit + insights to understand brand, audience, gaps, and growth levers.

Card 2 — Tailored Strategy

A modular plan covering exactly what you need: brand, positioning, SEO, CRO, content, funnels, etc.

Card 3 — Fast Implementation Support

Action steps, frameworks, and direction you can activate immediately.

Card 4 — Founder-Friendly Collaboration

Clear communication, async support, and a no-fluff, structured workflow.

Card 5 (Optional) — Data-Driven Decisions

Everything backed by research, market analysis, and performance signals.

Interaction idea:
Hover = micro animation / card “pops up”.
Tap on mobile = expands to show one-sentence explanation.
